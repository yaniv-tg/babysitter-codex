### Platform Differences

#### Navigation Patterns
- **iOS**: Tab bars at bottom, back button top-left
- **Android**: Navigation drawer, bottom nav, back button hardware/software
- **Cross-platform**: Adapt to platform conventions

#### Typography
- **iOS**: San Francisco font family
- **Android**: Roboto font family
- **Cross-platform**: Use platform-specific fonts or custom fonts

#### Gestures
- **iOS**: Swipe from left edge to go back
- **Android**: Back button, swipe from left/right edges
- **Cross-platform**: Support both patterns
