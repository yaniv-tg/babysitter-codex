# Nanotechnology - Process Backlog (Phase 2)

## Overview

This backlog contains concrete processes identified from the Nanotechnology specialization that can be orchestrated using the Babysitter SDK. Each process represents a repeatable workflow that can be automated, quality-gated, and iteratively refined. These processes cover nanomaterial synthesis, characterization, fabrication, computational modeling, and applications across medicine, electronics, and energy.

## Notes

- All processes should implement quality gates appropriate to their category and risk level
- Iterative refinement loops are essential for synthesis optimization and property targeting
- Integration with safety and regulatory requirements is mandatory for application-oriented processes
- Processes should maintain complete traceability from synthesis through characterization to application
- Reproducibility documentation and version control are critical for all experimental protocols
- Human-in-the-loop validation gates are required for safety assessments and regulatory submissions
- Cross-functional collaboration between synthesis, characterization, and computational teams is recommended
