# Civil Engineering Processes Backlog

## Overview

This document defines the Phase 2 processes backlog for the Civil Engineering specialization. These processes cover structural engineering, transportation systems, geotechnical analysis, water resources, and construction management workflows.

## Process Categories

### Structural Engineering Processes

#### 1. structural-load-analysis
- **Description**: Comprehensive load analysis process including dead loads, live loads, environmental loads (wind, seismic, snow, rain), and load combinations per ASCE 7
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: Building code requirements, site-specific data
- **Deliverables**: Load summary report, load combination tables, load path diagrams

#### 2. reinforced-concrete-design
- **Description**: Design of reinforced concrete structural elements per ACI 318 including beams, columns, slabs, walls, and foundations
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: structural-load-analysis
- **Deliverables**: Design calculations, reinforcement schedules, structural drawings

#### 3. structural-steel-design
- **Description**: Design of structural steel members and connections per AISC 360 including beams, columns, bracing, and moment frames
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: structural-load-analysis
- **Deliverables**: Member sizing calculations, connection designs, steel detailing drawings

#### 4. seismic-design-analysis
- **Description**: Earthquake-resistant design process including seismic hazard analysis, structural system selection, and compliance with ASCE 7 seismic provisions
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: structural-load-analysis, site geotechnical data
- **Deliverables**: Seismic design report, response spectrum analysis, detailing requirements

#### 5. bridge-design-lrfd
- **Description**: Bridge design process using AASHTO LRFD specifications including superstructure design, substructure design, and load rating
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Traffic data, geotechnical investigation
- **Deliverables**: Bridge design report, structural drawings, load rating documentation

### Geotechnical Engineering Processes

#### 6. geotechnical-site-investigation
- **Description**: Comprehensive subsurface investigation including boring program design, field testing, laboratory testing, and geotechnical report preparation
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: Site access, project requirements
- **Deliverables**: Geotechnical engineering report, boring logs, laboratory test results

#### 7. foundation-design
- **Description**: Design of shallow and deep foundation systems including spread footings, mat foundations, driven piles, and drilled shafts
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: geotechnical-site-investigation, structural-load-analysis
- **Deliverables**: Foundation design calculations, foundation plans, pile specifications

#### 8. slope-stability-analysis
- **Description**: Analysis of natural and engineered slopes including limit equilibrium analysis, factor of safety determination, and remediation design
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: geotechnical-site-investigation, topographic survey
- **Deliverables**: Slope stability report, analysis results, remediation recommendations

#### 9. retaining-wall-design
- **Description**: Design of earth retaining structures including gravity walls, cantilever walls, MSE walls, and soil nail walls
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: geotechnical-site-investigation, site grading plans
- **Deliverables**: Wall design calculations, structural drawings, construction specifications

### Transportation Engineering Processes

#### 10. highway-geometric-design
- **Description**: Geometric design of highways and roads per AASHTO Green Book including horizontal alignment, vertical alignment, and cross-sections
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Topographic survey, traffic analysis
- **Deliverables**: Geometric design report, plan and profile drawings, cross-section details

#### 11. traffic-impact-analysis
- **Description**: Analysis of traffic impacts from proposed development including trip generation, distribution, and intersection level of service analysis
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Traffic count data, development plans
- **Deliverables**: Traffic impact study, intersection analysis worksheets, mitigation recommendations

#### 12. pavement-design
- **Description**: Design of flexible and rigid pavement structures including traffic analysis, material selection, and thickness determination
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Traffic projections, subgrade soil properties
- **Deliverables**: Pavement design report, typical sections, material specifications

#### 13. intersection-signal-design
- **Description**: Design of traffic signals including signal timing, phasing, controller specifications, and detection systems per MUTCD
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Dependencies**: traffic-impact-analysis, intersection geometry
- **Deliverables**: Signal timing plans, wiring diagrams, equipment specifications

### Water Resources Engineering Processes

#### 14. stormwater-management-design
- **Description**: Design of stormwater management systems including hydrology analysis, detention design, conveyance systems, and LID/green infrastructure
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Site grading, watershed characteristics
- **Deliverables**: Stormwater management report, drainage plans, detention calculations

#### 15. hydraulic-structure-design
- **Description**: Design of hydraulic structures including culverts, channels, spillways, and energy dissipation structures
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Dependencies**: Hydrology analysis, site conditions
- **Deliverables**: Hydraulic calculations, structure drawings, specifications

#### 16. water-distribution-design
- **Description**: Design of water distribution systems including pipe network analysis, pump station design, and storage facility design
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Dependencies**: Demand projections, system hydraulics
- **Deliverables**: Distribution system plans, hydraulic model results, specifications

#### 17. flood-analysis-mitigation
- **Description**: Flood frequency analysis, floodplain mapping, and flood mitigation design including levees, floodwalls, and channel improvements
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Hydrologic data, topographic survey
- **Deliverables**: Flood study report, floodplain maps, mitigation design documents

### Construction Management Processes

#### 18. construction-cost-estimation
- **Description**: Development of construction cost estimates including quantity takeoffs, unit pricing, and contingency analysis
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: Critical
- **Dependencies**: Design documents, material pricing data
- **Deliverables**: Cost estimate report, quantity schedules, bid tabulations

#### 19. construction-schedule-development
- **Description**: Development of construction schedules using CPM including activity sequencing, resource loading, and critical path analysis
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Project scope, resource availability
- **Deliverables**: CPM schedule, Gantt charts, milestone reports

#### 20. construction-quality-control
- **Description**: Development and implementation of QA/QC programs including inspection protocols, testing requirements, and acceptance criteria
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Project specifications, testing standards
- **Deliverables**: QC plan, inspection checklists, testing protocols

#### 21. shop-drawing-review
- **Description**: Review of contractor shop drawings and submittals for compliance with design intent and specifications
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Design documents, contractor submittals
- **Deliverables**: Review comments, approval stamps, RFI responses

### Design Integration Processes

#### 22. bim-coordination
- **Description**: Building Information Modeling coordination including model development, clash detection, and multi-discipline integration
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Project BIM execution plan, discipline models
- **Deliverables**: Coordinated BIM model, clash reports, coordination drawings

#### 23. structural-peer-review
- **Description**: Independent peer review of structural design including code compliance verification, calculation checks, and constructability review
- **Complexity**: High
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Complete design documents
- **Deliverables**: Peer review report, comment resolution documentation

#### 24. specifications-development
- **Description**: Development of technical specifications for construction including CSI format organization, material requirements, and execution procedures
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Design documents, project requirements
- **Deliverables**: Project specifications manual, reference standard list

#### 25. permit-application-preparation
- **Description**: Preparation of permit applications including building permits, environmental permits, and regulatory agency submissions
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Priority**: High
- **Dependencies**: Design documents, site plans, environmental documentation
- **Deliverables**: Permit applications, supporting documentation, agency correspondence
