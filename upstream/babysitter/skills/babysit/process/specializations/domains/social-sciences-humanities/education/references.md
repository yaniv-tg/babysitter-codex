## Standards and Frameworks

### ISTE Standards

**Description**: Widely adopted standards for students, educators, and education leaders in technology-rich learning environments.

**Standards Sets**:
- ISTE Standards for Students
- ISTE Standards for Educators
- ISTE Standards for Education Leaders
- ISTE Standards for Coaches

**Access**: https://www.iste.org/standards

---

### Common Core State Standards

**Description**: Educational standards for English language arts and mathematics adopted by most U.S. states.

**Key Content**:
- English Language Arts standards
- Mathematics standards
- Literacy standards for history, science, and technical subjects
- Mathematical practices

**Access**: http://www.corestandards.org/

---

### Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)

**Description**: K-12 science content standards based on A Framework for K-12 Science Education.

**Key Features**:
- Three dimensions: practices, crosscutting concepts, disciplinary core ideas
- Performance expectations
- Progression across grade levels

**Access**: https://www.nextgenscience.org/

---

### Understanding by Design (UbD) Framework

**Description**: Curriculum design framework by Wiggins and McTighe emphasizing backward design.

**Key Concepts**:
- Backward design (three stages)
- Enduring understandings
- Essential questions
- WHERETO elements

**Resources**:
- [Understanding by Design](https://www.ascd.org/books/understanding-by-design-expanded-2nd-edition)
- [UbD in a Nutshell](https://www.ascd.org/el/articles/what-is-backward-design)

---

### Universal Design for Learning (UDL) Guidelines

**Description**: CAST framework for designing flexible learning experiences for all learners.

**Principles**:
- Multiple means of engagement
- Multiple means of representation
- Multiple means of action and expression

**Access**: https://udlguidelines.cast.org/

---

### Bloom's Taxonomy (Revised)

**Description**: Hierarchical framework for classifying educational learning objectives by cognitive complexity.

**Cognitive Levels**:
- Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyze, Evaluate, Create

**Resources**:
- [Vanderbilt Center for Teaching - Bloom's Taxonomy](https://cft.vanderbilt.edu/guides-sub-pages/blooms-taxonomy/)
- [Iowa State - Revised Bloom's Taxonomy](https://www.celt.iastate.edu/instructional-strategies/effective-teaching-practices/revised-blooms-taxonomy/)

---

### ADDIE Model

**Description**: Foundational instructional design model used across education and training contexts.

**Phases**:
- Analyze, Design, Develop, Implement, Evaluate

**Resources**:
- [ATD - ADDIE Model](https://www.td.org/talent-development-glossary-terms/what-is-addie)
- [Instructional Design - ADDIE](https://www.instructionaldesign.org/models/addie/)

---

### Quality Matters Rubric

**Description**: Research-based rubric for designing and evaluating online and blended courses.

**Standards Categories**:
- Course Overview and Introduction
- Learning Objectives
- Assessment and Measurement
- Instructional Materials
- Course Activities and Learner Interaction
- Course Technology
- Learner Support
- Accessibility and Usability

**Access**: https://www.qualitymatters.org/qa-resources/rubric-standards

---

### WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)

**Description**: International accessibility standard for digital content, essential for accessible learning materials.

**Principles**: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust (POUR)

**Access**: https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/

---

### Section 508 Standards

**Description**: U.S. federal accessibility requirements for electronic and information technology.

**Website**: https://www.section508.gov/

## Professional Certifications

### ATD Certifications

#### Associate Professional in Talent Development (APTD)

**Description**: Entry-level certification for talent development professionals.

**Requirements**:
- 3 years of work experience OR
- 2 years experience + education
- Pass certification exam

**Website**: https://www.td.org/certification/aptd

#### Certified Professional in Talent Development (CPTD)

**Description**: Advanced certification for experienced talent development professionals.
