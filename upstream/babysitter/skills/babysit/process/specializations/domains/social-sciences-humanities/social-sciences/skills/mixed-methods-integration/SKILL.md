# Mixed Methods Integration Skill

Design and execute mixed methods research integrating quantitative and qualitative approaches effectively.

## Overview

The Mixed Methods Integration skill enables design and execution of convergent, explanatory sequential, exploratory sequential, and other mixed methods research designs for comprehensive understanding through integration of quantitative and qualitative approaches.
