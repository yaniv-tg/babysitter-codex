# Cultural Policy Analysis

Analyze cultural policies, assess community cultural needs, and develop evidence-based recommendations for arts funding and cultural development.

## Overview

This skill enables systematic analysis of cultural policies and their impacts. It encompasses policy research, community assessment, stakeholder engagement, and evidence-based recommendation development to advance cultural development and arts funding priorities.

## Capabilities

### Policy Research
- Analyze existing cultural policies
- Compare policy approaches across jurisdictions
- Track legislative and regulatory developments
- Assess policy implementation and outcomes
- Identify policy gaps and opportunities

### Community Assessment
- Conduct cultural needs assessments
- Map community cultural assets
- Analyze demographic and participation data
- Engage diverse community voices
- Document cultural ecosystem dynamics

### Evidence Development
- Compile arts and cultural statistics
- Conduct economic impact studies
- Assess social outcomes of cultural programs
- Develop indicators and metrics
- Create compelling data visualizations

### Recommendation Development
- Frame policy options clearly
- Assess feasibility and impacts
- Consider equity implications
- Develop implementation strategies
- Build stakeholder support

## Usage Guidelines

### Analysis Process
1. Define policy questions and scope
2. Gather relevant data and evidence
3. Engage stakeholders for input
4. Analyze options and trade-offs
5. Develop recommendations
6. Communicate findings effectively

### Stakeholder Engagement
- Identify all relevant stakeholders
- Use appropriate consultation methods
- Ensure inclusive participation
- Document input systematically
- Report back on how input was used

### Documentation Standards
- Cite sources thoroughly
- Acknowledge limitations
- Present data accurately
- Make assumptions explicit
- Provide clear recommendations
