# Risk Mitigation Planning

Develop comprehensive risk management plans for collections and cultural venues including disaster preparedness, security protocols, and insurance coordination.

## Overview

This skill enables comprehensive risk management for cultural organizations. It encompasses risk assessment, disaster preparedness, security planning, and insurance coordination to protect collections, facilities, and personnel.

## Capabilities

### Risk Assessment
- Identify potential hazards and threats
- Assess probability and potential impact
- Prioritize risks systematically
- Map vulnerabilities in facilities and systems
- Document risk profiles

### Disaster Preparedness
- Develop emergency response plans
- Create evacuation procedures
- Establish salvage priorities
- Train staff on emergency response
- Conduct drills and exercises

### Security Planning
- Assess security vulnerabilities
- Develop access control protocols
- Plan surveillance and monitoring
- Create incident response procedures
- Coordinate with law enforcement

### Insurance Coordination
- Assess insurance needs and coverage
- Document collection values
- Coordinate appraisals and valuations
- Manage claims processes
- Review and update policies

## Usage Guidelines

### Risk Assessment Process
1. Identify all potential risks
2. Assess probability and impact
3. Prioritize based on risk matrix
4. Develop mitigation strategies
5. Implement controls and safeguards
6. Monitor and review regularly

### Emergency Planning
- Create clear command structures
- Establish communication protocols
- Identify evacuation routes and procedures
- Develop salvage and recovery plans
- Maintain emergency supplies and equipment

### Documentation Requirements
- Maintain current risk registers
- Document all procedures and protocols
- Keep emergency contact lists updated
- Archive incident reports
- Track insurance policies and coverage
