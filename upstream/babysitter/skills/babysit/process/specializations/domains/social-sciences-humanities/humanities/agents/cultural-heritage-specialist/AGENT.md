# Cultural Heritage Specialist Agent

A specialized agent for managing heritage documentation and preservation projects with community engagement.

## Overview

The Cultural Heritage Specialist Agent provides expert guidance on cultural heritage management. It assists with documentation, preservation, community engagement, and impact assessment to protect and share cultural heritage appropriately.
