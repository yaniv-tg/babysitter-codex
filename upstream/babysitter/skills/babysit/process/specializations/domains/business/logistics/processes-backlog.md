# Logistics and Operations - Processes Backlog

This document contains the prioritized backlog of AI-assisted processes for the Logistics and Operations specialization. Each process is designed to leverage AI capabilities to enhance logistics operations, optimize supply chains, and improve operational efficiency.

---

## Transportation Processes

- [ ] **Route Optimization** - AI-driven route planning and optimization to minimize transportation costs, reduce delivery times, and maximize vehicle utilization through dynamic routing algorithms. [Reference](https://developers.google.com/optimization/routing)

- [ ] **Carrier Selection and Procurement** - Automated carrier evaluation, rate comparison, and selection based on cost, service levels, capacity, and performance history to optimize freight spend. [Reference](https://www.ascm.org/learning-development/certifications-credentials/cltd/)

- [ ] **Freight Audit and Payment** - Automated freight bill validation, discrepancy identification, and payment processing to ensure billing accuracy and recover overcharges. [Reference](https://www.logisticsmgmt.com/)

- [ ] **Load Planning and Consolidation** - AI-powered load optimization to maximize trailer utilization, consolidate shipments, and reduce transportation costs through intelligent load building. [Reference](https://github.com/VROOM-Project/vroom)

- [ ] **Shipment Tracking and Visibility** - Real-time shipment monitoring, exception detection, and proactive customer communication through integrated visibility platforms. [Reference](https://www.project44.com/)

## Warehouse Processes

- [ ] **Slotting Optimization** - AI-driven warehouse slotting to optimize product placement based on velocity, pick frequency, ergonomics, and operational efficiency. [Reference](https://www.mheducation.com/highered/product/world-class-warehousing-material-handling-frazelle/M9780071376006.html)

- [ ] **Pick-Pack-Ship Operations** - Automated wave planning, pick path optimization, and packing validation to maximize warehouse throughput and order accuracy. [Reference](https://www.werc.org/)

- [ ] **Cycle Counting Program** - Systematic inventory counting process with AI-driven count scheduling, variance analysis, and root cause identification to maintain inventory accuracy. [Reference](https://www.inventoryops.com/books.htm)

- [ ] **Receiving and Putaway Optimization** - Streamlined inbound operations with dock scheduling, receipt validation, and optimized putaway location assignment. [Reference](https://www.koganpage.com/logistics-operations-management/warehouse-management-9781398614697)

- [ ] **Warehouse Labor Management** - AI-powered workforce planning, task assignment, and productivity optimization to maximize labor efficiency and meet service levels. [Reference](https://www.dcvelocity.com/)

## Inventory Processes

- [ ] **ABC-XYZ Analysis** - Multi-dimensional inventory classification combining value (ABC) and demand variability (XYZ) to set differentiated inventory policies and management strategies. [Reference](https://www.ascm.org/corporate-transformation/standards/scor/)

- [ ] **Reorder Point Calculation** - Dynamic safety stock and reorder point optimization based on demand variability, lead time analysis, and service level targets. [Reference](https://www.amazon.com/Demand-Driven-Material-Requirements-Planning-DDMRP/dp/0831136286)

- [ ] **FIFO-LIFO Inventory Control** - Automated inventory rotation management ensuring proper product flow based on expiration dates, production dates, or receipt dates. [Reference](https://www.fda.gov/food/guidance-regulation-food-and-dietary-supplements/food-safety-modernization-act-fsma)

- [ ] **Demand Forecasting** - AI-powered demand prediction using historical data, market signals, and external factors to improve forecast accuracy and inventory planning. [Reference](https://www.edx.org/micromasters/mitx-supply-chain-management)

- [ ] **Dead Stock and Excess Inventory Management** - Identification and disposition planning for slow-moving, obsolete, and excess inventory to optimize working capital. [Reference](https://www.amacombooks.org/book/essentials-of-inventory-management-third-edition/)
