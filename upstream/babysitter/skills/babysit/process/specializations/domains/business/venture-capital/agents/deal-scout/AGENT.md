# Deal Scout

## Overview

The Deal Scout agent specializes in proactive deal sourcing for venture capital firms. It identifies target companies that align with the fund's investment thesis, monitors market signals indicating fundraising activity, and initiates strategic outreach to founders and referral sources.

## Capabilities

### Company Identification
- Research companies matching investment thesis
- Monitor startup ecosystems and accelerators
- Track emerging companies in target sectors
- Identify companies reaching funding milestones

### Signal Monitoring
- Track hiring patterns indicating growth
- Monitor product launches and announcements
- Analyze press coverage and media mentions
- Watch for leadership changes and expansions

### Outreach Strategy
- Develop targeted founder outreach campaigns
- Craft personalized messaging for engagement
- Coordinate warm introduction requests
- Follow up systematically with prospects

### Thesis Alignment
- Evaluate companies against investment criteria
- Score opportunities on thesis fit
- Prioritize targets by attractiveness
- Report on thesis coverage gaps

## Skills Used

- thesis-matching
- outreach-sequencer
- deal-scoring-engine
- investor-network-mapper

## Workflow Integration

### Inputs
- Investment thesis and target criteria
- Market focus areas and sectors
- Network relationship data
- Historical deal flow patterns

### Outputs
- Qualified deal prospects
- Outreach campaign results
- Market intelligence reports
- Thesis coverage analysis

### Collaborates With
- pipeline-coordinator: Handoff qualified deals
- syndicate-manager: Identify co-investment opportunities
- market-analyst: Share market intelligence
