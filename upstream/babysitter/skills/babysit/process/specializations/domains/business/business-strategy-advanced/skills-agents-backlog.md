# Business Strategy Advanced - Skills and Agents Backlog (Phase 4)

## Overview

Skills and agents identified to support Business Strategy Advanced processes. This backlog captures specialized capabilities that go beyond general-purpose agents to provide deep expertise in strategic frameworks, competitive analysis, growth strategies, corporate strategy, and strategy execution methodologies used in modern business consulting and strategic planning.

## Identified Skills

- [ ] **porters-five-forces-skill** - Deep expertise in Porter's Five Forces framework, industry structure analysis, and competitive dynamics assessment
