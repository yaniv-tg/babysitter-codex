A curated collection of authoritative resources for Decision Intelligence, Business Intelligence, Competitive Intelligence, and Decision Science professionals.

## Books

### Decision Science and Decision Making

1. **Thinking, Fast and Slow**
   - Author: Daniel Kahneman
   - Publisher: Farrar, Straus and Giroux
   - URL: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/89308/thinking-fast-and-slow-by-daniel-kahneman/
   - Description: Nobel laureate's comprehensive exploration of the two systems that drive human thinking and decision-making, foundational for understanding cognitive biases.

2. **Predictably Irrational: The Hidden Forces That Shape Our Decisions**
   - Author: Dan Ariely
   - Publisher: Harper Perennial
   - URL: https://danariely.com/books/predictably-irrational/
   - Description: Exploration of systematic and predictable ways humans make irrational decisions, with implications for business decision-making.

3. **Nudge: Improving Decisions About Health, Wealth, and Happiness**
   - Authors: Richard H. Thaler and Cass R. Sunstein
   - Publisher: Penguin Books
   - URL: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/304634/nudge-by-richard-h-thaler-and-cass-r-sunstein/
   - Description: Foundational text on choice architecture and behavioral economics in decision design.

4. **The Art of Thinking Clearly**
   - Author: Rolf Dobelli
   - Publisher: Harper
   - URL: https://www.harpercollins.com/products/the-art-of-thinking-clearly-rolf-dobelli
   - Description: Practical guide to recognizing and avoiding cognitive biases in everyday and business decisions.

5. **Superforecasting: The Art and Science of Prediction**
   - Authors: Philip E. Tetlock and Dan Gardner
   - Publisher: Crown
   - URL: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/227815/superforecasting-by-philip-e-tetlock-and-dan-gardner/
   - Description: Research-based insights on how to improve judgment and forecasting accuracy.

6. **Smart Choices: A Practical Guide to Making Better Decisions**
   - Authors: John S. Hammond, Ralph L. Keeney, and Howard Raiffa
   - Publisher: Harvard Business Review Press
   - URL: https://hbr.org/product/smart-choices-a-practical-guide-to-making-better-decisions/8244-HBK-ENG
   - Description: Practical framework for structured decision-making from leading decision analysis experts.

7. **Decision Quality: Value Creation from Better Business Decisions**
   - Authors: Carl Spetzler, Hannah Winter, and Jennifer Meyer
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Decision+Quality%3A+Value+Creation+from+Better+Business+Decisions-p-9781119144670
   - Description: Comprehensive guide to the Decision Quality framework from Strategic Decisions Group.

8. **The Decision Book: Fifty Models for Strategic Thinking**
   - Authors: Mikael Krogerus and Roman Tschappeler
   - Publisher: W. W. Norton & Company
   - URL: https://wwnorton.com/books/The-Decision-Book/
   - Description: Collection of practical decision-making models and frameworks for business use.
