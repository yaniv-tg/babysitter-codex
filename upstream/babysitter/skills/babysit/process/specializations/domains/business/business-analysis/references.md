## Books

### Business Analysis Fundamentals

1. **A Guide to the Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK Guide) v3**
   - Author: International Institute of Business Analysis (IIBA)
   - Publisher: IIBA
   - URL: https://www.iiba.org/career-resources/babok-guide/
   - Description: The definitive global standard for business analysis practice, covering six knowledge areas and underlying competencies.

2. **Business Analysis Techniques: 99 Essential Tools for Success**
   - Authors: James Cadle, Debra Paul, and Paul Turner
   - Publisher: BCS, The Chartered Institute for IT
   - URL: https://shop.bcs.org/store/bcs/product/business-analysis-techniques
   - Description: Comprehensive toolkit of techniques for investigation, strategy analysis, process modeling, and requirements engineering.

3. **Business Analysis: Best Practices for Success**
   - Author: Steven P. Blais
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Business+Analysis:+Best+Practices+for+Success-p-9780470139103
   - Description: Practical guide to business analysis covering the full project lifecycle from inception to implementation.

4. **Writing Better Requirements**
   - Author: Ian Alexander and Ljerka Beus-Dukic
   - Publisher: Addison-Wesley
   - URL: https://www.informit.com/store/writing-better-requirements-9780321636485
   - Description: Essential guide to writing clear, unambiguous, and testable requirements.

5. **User Stories Applied: For Agile Software Development**
   - Author: Mike Cohn
   - Publisher: Addison-Wesley
   - URL: https://www.mountaingoatsoftware.com/books/user-stories-applied
   - Description: Foundational text on writing effective user stories for agile development.
