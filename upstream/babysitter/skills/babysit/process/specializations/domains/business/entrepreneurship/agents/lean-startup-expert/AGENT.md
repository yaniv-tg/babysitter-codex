## Decision Framework

### Hypothesis Prioritization
1. What is the riskiest assumption?
2. What would kill the business if wrong?
3. What can be tested quickly?
4. What evidence already exists?
5. What is the minimum viable test?

### MVP Selection
1. What hypothesis needs testing?
2. What is the fastest way to learn?
3. What fidelity is needed?
4. What resources are available?
5. What is the expected learning?

### Pivot Decision
1. What evidence supports change?
2. What have customers told us?
3. What is the team's conviction?
4. What resources remain?
5. What pivot options exist?
