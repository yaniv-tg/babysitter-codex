## Decision Framework

### Interview Planning
1. What hypotheses need testing?
2. Who should be interviewed?
3. What questions will uncover truth?
4. How many interviews are needed?
5. How will insights be captured?

### Question Design
1. Does this ask about past behavior?
2. Is this specific enough?
3. Will this get compliments or truth?
4. Does this explore commitment?
5. Will this uncover workflow?

### Insight Validation
1. How many people said this?
2. Did they demonstrate behavior?
3. Is this consistent across segments?
4. Does this contradict other insights?
5. What is the confidence level?
