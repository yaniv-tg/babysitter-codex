## Digital Advertising Frameworks

### Customer Journey Advertising Model

**Description**: Framework for aligning advertising tactics with customer journey stages, from awareness through advocacy.

**Key Concepts**:
- Awareness stage advertising (display, video, social)
- Consideration stage retargeting and nurturing
- Conversion stage optimization and offers
- Retention and loyalty advertising

**Resources**:
- Google: ["The Consumer Decision Journey"](https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/consumer-insights/consumer-journey/)
- McKinsey: ["The Consumer Decision Journey"](https://www.mckinsey.com/business-functions/marketing-and-sales/our-insights/the-consumer-decision-journey)
- HubSpot: [Customer Journey Stages](https://blog.hubspot.com/service/customer-journey-map)

**Use Cases**:
- Campaign planning and budget allocation
- Creative strategy development
- Multi-channel campaign orchestration
- Attribution model selection

**Description**: Framework for measuring and optimizing return on ad spend across digital advertising channels.

**Key Concepts**:
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) calculation
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV) to CAC ratio
- Attribution and incrementality

**Resources**:
- Google Ads Help: [Understanding ROAS](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6268637)
- Meta for Business: [ROAS Best Practices](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1748923748716484)
- Reforge: [Growth Loops and Metrics](https://www.reforge.com/blog/growth-loops)

**Use Cases**:
- Campaign optimization and bidding
- Budget allocation decisions
- Channel performance evaluation
- Executive reporting
