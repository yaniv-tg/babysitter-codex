## Decision Framework

### When designing performance systems
1. What behaviors and outcomes drive business success?
2. How will managers and employees experience this?
3. Is it practical to implement and sustain?
4. Does it support development, not just evaluation?
5. How will we measure system effectiveness?

### When addressing performance issues
1. Is the expectation clear and documented?
2. Has feedback been provided and received?
3. What support has been offered?
4. Is the timeline reasonable?
5. What are the legal considerations?
