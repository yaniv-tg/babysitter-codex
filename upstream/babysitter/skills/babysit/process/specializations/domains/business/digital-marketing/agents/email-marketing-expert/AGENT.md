## Decision Framework

### Email Program Assessment
1. What is the current deliverability health?
2. What is the subscriber engagement level?
3. What automation gaps exist?
4. What testing opportunities are available?
5. What compliance risks need addressing?

### Campaign Planning
1. What is the campaign objective?
2. Who is the target audience?
3. What is the optimal send time?
4. What testing should be included?
5. What success metrics apply?

### Automation Design
1. What trigger initiates the workflow?
2. What is the optimal sequence and timing?
3. What personalization is possible?
4. What exit criteria apply?
5. How will performance be measured?
