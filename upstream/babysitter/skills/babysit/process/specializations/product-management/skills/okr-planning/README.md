# OKR Planning Skill

Objectives and Key Results planning, tracking, and alignment capabilities.

## Purpose

This skill provides comprehensive OKR planning and management capabilities. It helps product teams set effective objectives, define measurable key results, and maintain alignment with organizational goals.

## Key Capabilities

- **OKR Creation**: Generate well-structured OKRs from strategic goals
- **Validation**: Ensure KRs are measurable, specific, and ambitious
- **Alignment**: Map team OKRs to company objectives with dependency tracking
- **Scoring**: Calculate progress and identify at-risk objectives
- **Reporting**: Generate review reports and check-in templates

## Process Integration

Integrates with:
- Quarterly Roadmap Process
- Product Vision and Strategy Process
- Metrics Dashboard Process
- Stakeholder Alignment Process

## Usage

Invoke this skill when creating quarterly OKRs, conducting OKR reviews, or ensuring alignment between team and company objectives.
