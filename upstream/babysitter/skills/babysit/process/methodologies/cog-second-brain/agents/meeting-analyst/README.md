# Meeting Analyst Agent

Processes meeting recordings and transcripts into structured intelligence.

## Overview

The Meeting Analyst turns unstructured meeting content into actionable outputs: decisions with context, action items with owners, and team dynamics analysis.

## Use Cases

- **Transcript Processing** - Parse recordings into structured, searchable content
- **Decision Extraction** - Identify decisions with rationale and stakeholders
- **Action Item Tracking** - Extract commitments with owners and deadlines
- **Meeting Summaries** - Generate executive-level meeting overviews
- **Topic Segmentation** - Break transcripts into discussion threads

## Key Principle

Structured extraction: every meeting output should be actionable. Decisions link to projects, action items have owners, and summaries highlight what matters.
